FIRST COPY FOR
THE FIRST CITIZEN

April 10, 2003, was a red-letter day for **Chandamama**, when the First copy of the first issue of **Junior Chandamama** was presented to His Excellency **Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam**, President of India, who has endeared himself as a trusted friend of the children of India. He graciously accepted the magazine from the publishers who had called on him that evening. He was very appreciative of the publication and blessed the bold venture, launched after providing healthy reading for children for 56 years.
